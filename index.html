    Hello and welcome to The World Prayer Map.

    This project started as an internal tool to help our church creative team design prayer graphics more efficiently. Over time, it grew into an interactive resource for creating country-specific prayer points and visuals.

    In 2024, our church began praying over one nation each week, and we recently completed our 90th nation. Through that process, we saw the need for a simple, organized tool that could help churches focus their prayers, build awareness, and create resources that support intentional prayer for the nations.

    The World Prayer Map is designed for churches, prayer teams, missions teams, small groups, classrooms, and creative teams who want a clear and practical way to pray for countries around the world.

    The goal is simple: to help the church pray with greater focus, consistency, and understanding as we intercede for the nations.
